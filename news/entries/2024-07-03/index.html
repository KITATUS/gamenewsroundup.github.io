EA is "sunsetting" three last-gen Battlefield games

  • Battlefield 3 (Xbox 360 & PlayStation 3), Battlefield 4 (Xbox 360 & PlayStation 3), Battlefield: Hardline (Xbox 360 & PlayStation 3)
  • On Wednesday, July 31st they will be removed from digital storefronts and you will no longer be able to purchase them and all extra related content such as DLC
  • Online services go offline November 7th 2024.
  • Battlefield 3 will still be available for play on PC. Battlefield 4, and Battlefield: Hardline will still be available for play on PC, Xbox One, and PlayStation 4.

Nintendo sues Switch modchip seller and Switch piracy Reddit moderator
