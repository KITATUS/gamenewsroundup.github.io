PlayStation 5 "most profitable generation to date", Sony says

  • Operating income for PS5 gen reaches $10bn, beating PS4's $9bn

  • Of the 3.8bn hours played, 2.4bn of those were on PS5
  • PS5 users are spending 176 percent more on add-on content, 57 percent more on services and 34 percent more on peripherals than the previous generation of console did in the first four years of its cycle
  • PS5 users are also spending 12 percent less on full game content than those on PS4 did
