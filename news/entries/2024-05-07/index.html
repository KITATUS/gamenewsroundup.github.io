Remedy has cancelled "Project Kestral"

  • According to Remedy Entertainment, the cancelation of “Kestrel” (previously "Vanguard") will allow other ongoing game development projects to gain experienced developers reassigned from “Kestrel.”
  • Additionally, investment needs for the project have been removed, and Remedy Entertainment’s overall recruitment needs have been reduced.

Hades 2 Early access is out now

  • Available in early access on Steam and Epic Games Store
  • The full release date of the final game has not been given at this time

Nintendo confirms Switch Successor will be announced "this fiscal year"

  • A Switch Nintendo Direct to be held this June
  • Don't expect Switch Successor news at the Nintendo Direct

Beat Saber drops Meta Quest 1 support in November

  • On November 2, Multiplayer will be disabled for the game on Meta Quest 1 (but the game will remain playable)
  • Leaderboards "might" be shut off later down the line.

Story Kitchen teams with Dreamworks Animation to adapt video games to film and TV

  • The Kung Fu Panda studio has a first-look option on any game-based animated movie Story Kitchen works on.
  • Story Kitchen is currently working on a live-action Dredge film and Vampire Survivors animated series (They aren't included in this deal)
  • Story Kitchen's noted projects as of late include an adaptation of It Takes Two for Amazon, Lionsgate's Streets of Rage movie, and Netflix's Tomb Raider anime
