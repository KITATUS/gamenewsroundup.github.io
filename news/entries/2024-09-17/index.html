Italian police smash counterfeit retro games ring worth £42m (€50m/$55.5m)

  • The pirated material included 12,000 counterfeit "Nintendo, Sega, and Atari" consoles holding 47m fake versions of retro games like Mario Bros., Street Fighter, and Star Wars.

  • The game will ditch the 128-player matches and specialists featured in Battlefield 2042
  • While EA hasn’t confirmed a launch window for the game, Respawn and Infinity Ward co-founder Vince Zampella said some players will be able to sample it in some form next year.

IBM wins $44.9m in infringement lawsuit against Zynga

  • Jury found in favour of IBM, which says Zynga's Farmville game breached patents
  • IBM convinced a Delaware jury that mobile developer Zynga infringed its web-based communications patents with its hugely successful mobile game, Farmville, as well as several spin-off titles.
  • Zynga owner Take Two says the patents are now expired and that it will appeal (they weren't expired when the supposed infringing took place)

VR studio nDreams is set to lay off up to 17.5% of its staff

  • "For now, our priority is to support our people in this process"
  • On LinkedIn, nDreams lists its headcount as between 201 and 500 employees, intimating anywhere between 35 and 88 jobs are at risk at "all levels" of the organisation.
