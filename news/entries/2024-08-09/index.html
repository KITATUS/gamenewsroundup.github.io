Doom 1 + 2 have be re-released yet AGAIN. This time combined and with a new episode

  • People who owned Doom or Doom 2 on Steam, PSN, Xbox, eShop or GOG can get the enhanced edition at no extra cost (automatically upgraded).
  • Bundle includes: DOOM, DOOM 2, TNT: Evilution, The Plutonia Experiment, Master Levels for Doom 2, No Rest for the Living, Sigil, Legacy of Rust, Deathmatch pack (26 maps)
  • All in all, there are a total of 187 mission maps and 43 deathmatch maps
  • Now on the KEX engine
  • Players will also be able to choose between the original soundtrack, or a new arranged soundtrack by Andrew Hulshult, who composed the music for Brutal Doom and co-composed the music for the Ancient Gods expansion for Doom Eternal.
  • The new edition also supports cross-platform multiplayer for up to 16 players in both Deathmatch and co-op mode.
