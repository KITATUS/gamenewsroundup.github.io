Antstream Arcade is coming to PlayStation consoles

  • The subscription service lets players access a streaming library of over 1,300 retro games.
  • The full list of games available currently stands at 1,357, including 200+ Amiga games, nearly 300 arcade games, 350+ C64 games, 50+ Mega Drive games, 40+ SNES games and 350+ ZX Spectrum games.
  • Players can either buy a year’s subscription to the service for $39.99 / £39.99, or a Lifetime Pass Edition for $99.99 / £99.99.
  • It was also announced that the PS1 Net Yaroze games will soon be coming to Antstream, while Pico-8 developers can now submit their games to be included on the service. The first Pico-8 games will be Puzzles of the Paladin and Manbomber.
  • Release date TBA

Supercell appoints new CFO

  • Supercell has appointed Gerard Griffin as its new CFO.
  • Griffin has over 25 years of experience in finance and business leadership roles, having previously assumed senior positions at firms like EA, Zynga, NBC, and Sportradar.
  • Outgoing CFO Janne Snellman will remain at Supercell "with a focus on supporting existing and future growth initiatives like working with investee companies."
