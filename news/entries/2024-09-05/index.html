Ubisoft’s share price has fallen to a near 10-year low since the release of Star Wars Outlaws

  • Share Price: €15.20, lowest since 2015.
  • Historical High: €102.95 in July 2018.
  • Sales Expectations: Revised from 7.5 million to 5.5 million units by March 2025.
VGC

Riot adjusts regional pricing of virtual currency across its live games

  • Riot Games is altering regional pricing of its virtual currency, with one country seeing increases over 70%.
  • Announced yesterday, the developer said these changes have been made in order to "better balance prices between regions."
  • The United States will see no change in pricing, while Ukraine will experience increases of between 64% and 74% across all games. For League of Legends and Teamfight Tactics specifically, the Philippines will see a 40% rise while Vietnam will observe a 41% increase.
