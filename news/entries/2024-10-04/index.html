Hi-Rez Studios lays off staff to 'ensure' Smite 2's long-term success

  • Several teams across Hi-Rez and its subsidiaries have been impacted by layoffs as the company puts more weight behind Smite 2's development.
  • A specific number of reductions was not revealed, but Chisam said Hi-Rez's marketing and publishing teams have been affected. Cosmetic content and system features teams at subsidiaries Evil Mojo (Paladins), G&A, and Titan Forge (Smite) were also impacted.

  • "Our proven model puts us in pole position to capture the global UGC market," said co-founder
