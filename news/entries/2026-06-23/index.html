Valve has revealed the price of the Steam Machine, ahead of its release

  • 512GB model is $1,049 / £879
  • 512GB with Steam Controller is $1,128 / £938
  • 2TB model is $1,349 / £1,149
  • 2TB with Steam Controller is $1,428 / £1,208
  • Users are able to register their interest for any of the Steam Machine models from now until June 25, at which point all pre-orders will be randomised and placed into a queue
  • Initial reaction has been negative, citing poor performance to price
