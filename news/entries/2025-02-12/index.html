Royal Shakespeare Company collaborating on first-ever video game adaptation with former GTA developer

  • The Royal Shakespeare Company is working on its first-ever video game, an adaptation of Macbeth set in modern day Iran
  • Navid Khonsari previously worked at GTA developer Rockstar as cinematic director on GTA 3, Vice City and San Andreas, as well as the Max Payne games. After leaving the company he co-founded iNK Stories with his wife Vassiliki Khonsari and together they gained notoriety for their previous BAFTA-nominated game 1979 Revolution: Black Friday set in the Iranian Revolution.

Roblox revenues rose 29% during 2024

  • Revenue: $988.2 million, up 32% year-on-year
