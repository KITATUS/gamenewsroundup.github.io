Gearbox, Bethesda and Epic veterans reveal their new multiplayer ‘suburban invasion’ game

  • Ruckus Games raises $19m in funding round led by Krafton. Bitkraft Ventures, Transcend, and Hypergryph also contributed to the round, among others.
  • Ruckus was founded in 2021 by Gearbox and Riot alumni, and is led by CEO Paul Sage, formerly creative director on Borderlands 3.
  • The currently unnamed project is described as a “session-based suburban invasion game combining RPG elements, crafting, town building and more”.
