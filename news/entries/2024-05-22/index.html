Business4 stories

PlayStation London Studio has officially closed its doors

  • Founded in 2002 and based in Soho, London, the first-party studio worked on The Getaway series and was responsible for the Singstar franchise.
  • It also developed numerous games for PlayStation’s EyeToy camera, Wonderbook, and PlayStation VR titles VR Worlds and Blood & Truth.
VGC

IGN Entertainment acquires Eurogamer, GI, VG247, Rock Paper Shotgun and more

  • New owner makes redundancies across the Gamer Network portfolio
  • Gamer Network’s publications are GamesIndustry.biz, Eurogamer (including six local language editions), Rock Paper Shotgun, VG247 and Dicebreaker.
  • The business also holds shares in Outside Xbox, Digital Foundry and Hookshot (which operates Nintendolife, PushSquare, PureXbox and Time Extension).
GamesIndustry.biz

Amazon Games is opening its first European studio, helmed by a Ubisoft veteran

  • Amazon Games Bucharest
  • The studio will be led by Cristian Pana, former managing director at Ubisoft Bucharest.
  • Pana has over 20 years of experience and previously worked on Far Cry, The Division, and Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora.
