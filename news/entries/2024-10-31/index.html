Two box sets celebrating the games of Tetsuya Mizuguchi announced

  • Sounds and Visions: Enhance at 10 and The Enhance Soundworks Collection Boxed Set are available to pre-order now ahead of their release next year.
  • Produced by Enhance, Lost in Cult, and Cook & Becker, Sounds and Visions: Enhance at 10 and The Enhance Soundworks Collection Boxed Set will be limited to 1010 and 505 copies respectively.
  • The Sounds & Visions: Enhance at 10 Boxed Set contains a book, CD, glass sculpture and glass prints
  • The Enhance Soundworks Collection Boxed Set features 9 Vinyl Records covering Rez Infinite, Lumines, Tetris Effect and Humanity

Nintendo Music app launching later today, featuring decades of Nintendo soundtracks

  • Exclusive to Nintendo Switch Online members (basic tier)
  • For iOS and Android
  • Due to the way video-game songs are constructed, some songs can be "extended" (looped) for a custom set user time period (up to 60 minutes)
  • Users will be given recommendations based on their Nintendo Switch play history, and it will also be possible to hide certain soundtracks in order to avoid spoilers.
  • More songs will be added to the future
  • Fan response has been mixed. The app itself has been praised but the "fear" Nintendo will start copyrighting music videos on YouTube has caused an overall negative reaction to the news
