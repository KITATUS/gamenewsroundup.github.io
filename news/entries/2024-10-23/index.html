Warhammer 40K: Space Marine 2 getting an animated sequel in Amazon's new Secret Level series

Those games include Armored Core, Concord, Crossfire, Dungeons & Dragons, Exodus, Honor of Kings, Mega Man, New World: Aeternum, Pac-Man, Sifu, Spelunky, The Outer Worlds, Unreal Tournament, a variety of PlayStation Studios games, and Warhammer 40K.

Sea of Thieves temporarily yanks nearly all its big new stealth features following "significant issues"

  • Just days after the launch of Sea of Thieves' stealth-focused Season 14, developer Rare has yanked nearly all its new features - including the ability to crouch and don disguises - acknowledging "significant issues" arising from their addition to the game.

Bungie creative team joins PlayStation to support live service games

  • Bridget O’Neill, who previously served as senior director of Bungie Creative Studios, discussed the restructuring in a LinkedIn update this week: “Today was my team’s last day at Bungie, but not our last day working on Destiny (and Marathon), Bungie Creative Studios is joining PlayStation Studios to build the foundation for a creative team that can support all PlayStation live service games. As part of this move, I am taking on a new role as Senior Director of Creative at PlayStation Studios.”
  • The studio previously said it would integrate 155 roles into Sony Interactive Entertainment

Before I get messages about the Prince of Persia story floating around - it is being misreported. Ubisoft Montpellier does not seem to be closing down - the project has concluded and the team is being moved onto to different projects. The headline that the team has been closed is not an accurate headline.
