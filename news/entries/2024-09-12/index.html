Steam’s improved family sharing is out now for everyone

  • The Steam Families feature lets you share games with multiple people, manage parental controls, and approve requests to buy games.
  • Up to five family members can be invited into a group, which will grant access to a shared family library. Each person can create their own save files and collect their own achievements for games in the family library
  • The marquee development with the new family feature is that multiple people within the group will be able to play games from the shared library at the same time. And if you own multiple copies of a game, then multiple people can be playing that title at once. The caveat with family sharing is that a game developer may opt not to support the feature

Payday 3’s director has been demoted following the game’s troubled first year

  • Former game director Miodrag Kovačević is now serving as a senior game designer on the project, according to his LinkedIn profile, while Andreas Häll Penninger and Almir Listo will be “the creative force behind the upcoming updates”.
  • Penninger was a lead producer on Payday 3 and a producer on its predecessor, while Listo is global brand director and head of community for the series.
