Hi-Fi Rush studio Tango Gameworks has been acquired by Krafton and will remain open

  • Unclear specifically how many staff will be coming along as part of the deal, seeing as the studio was originally shut down in late May.
  • Hi-Fi Rush is the only IP coming along with the deal. The Evil Within series and Ghostwire Tokyo remain with Microsoft.
  • Krafton said that the acquisition will have no impact on the availability of Tango's previous releases, and that they will remain available where they are currently.
