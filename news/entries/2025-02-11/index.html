Marvel Rivals rolls back controversial mid-season rank reset hours after announcement

  • Last night, it was announced in 10 days that players would have a rank reset part-way through the current season of Marvel Rivals.
  • After extremely negative feedback, Netease has rolled back the decision mere hours after the announcement.
  • Instead of a rank reset, players will have to to play 10 matches to redeem the rewards for their rank following the mid-season update.
